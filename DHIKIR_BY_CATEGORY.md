## Emotional States

### 💫 anxious
**Anxious** (قلق) — *Find peace and tranquility*

#### 1. Sufficient for us is Allah, and He is the best Disposer of affairs

> حَسْبُنَا اللَّهُ وَنِعْمَ الْوَكِيلُ

**Transliteration:** *Hasbunallahu wa ni'mal wakeel*

**Source:** Quran 3:173 (Quran) | **Repeat:** 7x

📖 Said by Prophet Ibrahim (AS) when thrown into fire, and by the Companions before the Battle of Badr. Brings immense peace during anxiety and fear.

---

#### 2. There is no deity except You; exalted are You. Indeed, I have been of the wrongdoers

> لَا إِلَٰهَ إِلَّا أَنتَ سُبْحَانَكَ إِنِّي كُنتُ مِنَ الظَّالِمِينَ

**Transliteration:** *La ilaha illa anta subhanaka inni kuntu minaz-zalimin*

**Source:** Quran 21:87 (Quran) | **Repeat:** 3x

📖 The supplication of Prophet Yunus (AS) in the belly of the whale. Allah says no Muslim will call upon Him with this except that He will respond.

---

#### 3. O Allah, I seek refuge in You from worry and grief, from weakness and laziness, from cowardice and miserliness, and from being overcome by debt and overpowered by men

> اللَّهُمَّ إِنِّي أَعُوذُ بِكَ مِنَ الْهَمِّ وَالْحَزَنِ، وَأَعُوذُ بِكَ مِنَ الْعَجْزِ وَالْكَسَلِ، وَأَعُوذُ بِكَ مِنَ الْجُبْنِ وَالْبُخْلِ، وَأَعُوذُ بِكَ مِنْ غَلَبَةِ الدَّيْنِ وَقَهْرِ الرِّجَالِ

**Transliteration:** *Allahumma inni a'udhu bika minal-hammi wal-hazan, wa a'udhu bika minal-'ajzi wal-kasal, wa a'udhu bika minal-jubni wal-bukhl, wa a'udhu bika min ghalabatid-dayni wa qahrir-rijal*

**Source:** Sahih Bukhari 6369 (Hadith) | **Repeat:** 3x

📖 The Prophet (SAW) used to frequently make this comprehensive dua seeking refuge from worry, sadness, and all forms of distress.

---

#### 4. I seek refuge in Allah from Satan, the accursed

> أَعُوذُ بِاللَّهِ مِنَ الشَّيْطَانِ الرَّجِيمِ

**Transliteration:** *A'udhu billahi minash-shaytanir-rajim*

**Source:** Quran 16:98 (Quran) | **Repeat:** 3x

📖 The Prophet (SAW) advised saying this when angry. Seeking refuge from Shaytan helps calm the heart and dispel anger.

---

#### 5. O Allah, there is no ease except what You make easy, and You make the difficulty easy if You wish

> اللَّهُمَّ لَا سَهْلَ إِلَّا مَا جَعَلْتَهُ سَهْلًا، وَأَنْتَ تَجْعَلُ الْحَزْنَ إِذَا شِئْتَ سَهْلًا

**Transliteration:** *Allahumma la sahla illa ma ja'altahu sahla, wa anta taj'alul-hazna idha shi'ta sahla*

**Source:** Sahih Ibn Hibban 2427 (Hadith) | **Repeat:** 7x

📖 A powerful dua for when facing any difficulty. Reminds us that only Allah can make things easy.

---

#### 6. O Ever-Living, O Sustainer, in Your mercy I seek relief

> يَا حَيُّ يَا قَيُّومُ بِرَحْمَتِكَ أَسْتَغِيثُ

**Transliteration:** *Ya Hayyu ya Qayyumu bi rahmatika astaghith*

**Source:** Jami at-Tirmidhi 3524 (Hadith) | **Repeat:** 3x

📖 The Prophet (SAW) taught this dua for times of distress. Calling upon Allah by His beautiful names.

---

#### 7. My Lord, expand for me my chest and ease for me my task

> رَبِّ اشْرَحْ لِي صَدْرِي وَيَسِّرْ لِي أَمْرِي

**Transliteration:** *Rabbish-rahli sadri wa yassirli amri*

**Source:** Quran 20:25-26 (Quran) | **Repeat:** 3x

📖 The dua of Prophet Musa (AS) before speaking to Pharaoh. Excellent for expanding the chest and easing tasks.

---

#### 8. In the name of Allah, I place my trust in Allah. There is no power or strength except with Allah

> بِسْمِ اللَّهِ، تَوَكَّلْتُ عَلَى اللَّهِ، لَا حَوْلَ وَلَا قُوَّةَ إِلَّا بِاللَّهِ

**Transliteration:** *Bismillah, tawakkaltu 'alallah, la hawla wa la quwwata illa billah*

**Source:** Sunan Abu Dawud 5095 (Hadith) | **Repeat:** 1x

📖 Said when leaving the house. One who says this is guided, protected, and defended, and Satan will stay away.

---

#### 9. O Allah, I seek refuge in You from laziness and old age, from sin and debt

> اللَّهُمَّ إِنِّي أَعُوذُ بِكَ مِنَ الْكَسَلِ وَالْهَرَمِ، وَالْمَأْثَمِ وَالْمَغْرَمِ

**Transliteration:** *Allahumma inni a'udhu bika minal-kasali wal-haram, wal-ma'thami wal-maghram*

**Source:** Sahih Bukhari 6368 (Hadith) | **Repeat:** 3x

📖 The Prophet (SAW) frequently sought refuge from these four things that weaken a person.

---

#### 10. There is no power or strength except with Allah

> لَا حَوْلَ وَلَا قُوَّةَ إِلَّا بِاللَّهِ

**Transliteration:** *La hawla wa la quwwata illa billah*

**Source:** Sahih Bukhari 6384 (Hadith) | **Repeat:** 10x

📖 One of the treasures from beneath the Throne of Allah. Said when feeling powerless or overwhelmed.

---

#### 11. O Turner of hearts, make my heart firm upon Your religion

> يَا مُقَلِّبَ الْقُلُوبِ ثَبِّتْ قَلْبِي عَلَى دِينِكَ

**Transliteration:** *Ya Muqallibal-qulub, thabbit qalbi 'ala dinik*

**Source:** Jami at-Tirmidhi 2140 (Hadith) | **Repeat:** 7x

📖 The Prophet (SAW) made this dua frequently. Our hearts are between Allah's fingers, and He turns them as He wills.

---

#### 12. Our Lord, pour upon us patience and plant firmly our feet and give us victory over the disbelieving people

> رَبَّنَا أَفْرِغْ عَلَيْنَا صَبْرًا وَثَبِّتْ أَقْدَامَنَا وَانصُرْنَا عَلَى الْقَوْمِ الْكَافِرِينَ

**Transliteration:** *Rabbana afrigh 'alayna sabran wa thabbit aqdamana wansurna 'alal-qawmil-kafirin*

**Source:** Quran 2:250 (Quran) | **Repeat:** 3x

📖 The dua of the believers when facing Goliath. Asking for patience and steadfastness.

---

#### 13. O Allah, I seek refuge in You from the withdrawal of Your blessing, the change of Your protection, Your sudden punishment, and all Your displeasure

> اللَّهُمَّ إِنِّي أَعُوذُ بِكَ مِنْ زَوَالِ نِعْمَتِكَ، وَتَحَوُّلِ عَافِيَتِكَ، وَفُجَاءَةِ نِقْمَتِكَ، وَجَمِيعِ سَخَطِكَ

**Transliteration:** *Allahumma inni a'udhu bika min zawali ni'matik, wa tahawwuli 'afiyatik, wa fuja'ati niqmatik, wa jami'i sakhatik*

**Source:** Sahih Muslim 2739 (Hadith) | **Repeat:** 3x

📖 A comprehensive dua seeking protection from losing Allah's favors and blessings.

---

#### 14. Our Lord, forgive us our sins and our excess in our affairs and plant firmly our feet and give us victory

> رَبَّنَا اغْفِرْ لَنَا ذُنُوبَنَا وَإِسْرَافَنَا فِي أَمْرِنَا وَثَبِّتْ أَقْدَامَنَا وَانصُرْنَا عَلَى الْقَوْمِ الْكَافِرِينَ

**Transliteration:** *Rabbanaghfir lana dhunubana wa israfana fi amrina wa thabbit aqdamana wansurna 'alal-qawmil-kafirin*

**Source:** Quran 3:147 (Quran) | **Repeat:** 3x

📖 The dua of the righteous warriors who fought alongside the prophets. Combines repentance with asking for steadfastness.

---

#### 15. O Allah, I seek refuge in You from poverty, scarcity, humiliation, and from wronging others or being wronged

> اللَّهُمَّ إِنِّي أَعُوذُ بِكَ مِنَ الْفَقْرِ، وَالْقِلَّةِ، وَالذِّلَّةِ، وَأَعُوذُ بِكَ مِنْ أَنْ أَظْلِمَ أَوْ أُظْلَمَ

**Transliteration:** *Allahumma inni a'udhu bika minal-faqr, wal-qillah, wadh-dhillah, wa a'udhu bika min an azlima aw uzlam*

**Source:** Sunan Abu Dawud 1544 (Hadith) | **Repeat:** 3x

📖 Seeking protection from hardships that affect dignity and from injustice in any form.

---

#### 16. O Allah, I seek refuge in You from the evil of my hearing, my sight, my tongue, and my heart

> اللَّهُمَّ إِنِّي أَعُوذُ بِكَ مِنْ شَرِّ سَمْعِي، وَمِنْ شَرِّ بَصَرِي، وَمِنْ شَرِّ لِسَانِي، وَمِنْ شَرِّ قَلْبِي

**Transliteration:** *Allahumma inni a'udhu bika min sharri sam'i, wa min sharri basari, wa min sharri lisani, wa min sharri qalbi*

**Source:** Sunan Abu Dawud 1551 (Hadith) | **Repeat:** 3x

📖 Seeking protection from sins committed through our senses. The gateway to most sins.

---

#### 17. O Allah, suffice me with what You have made lawful against what You have made unlawful, and enrich me by Your favor against all others

> اللَّهُمَّ اكْفِنِي بِحَلَالِكَ عَنْ حَرَامِكَ، وَأَغْنِنِي بِفَضْلِكَ عَمَّنْ سِوَاكَ

**Transliteration:** *Allahummak-fini bi-halalika 'an haramik, wa aghnini bi-fadlika 'amman siwak*

**Source:** Jami at-Tirmidhi 3563 (Hadith) | **Repeat:** 7x

📖 A powerful dua for provision and protection from haram. The Prophet (SAW) taught this to those in debt.

---

#### 18. My Lord, I seek refuge in You from the incitements of the devils, and I seek refuge in You, my Lord, from their presence

> رَبِّ أَعُوذُ بِكَ مِنْ هَمَزَاتِ الشَّيَاطِينِ وَأَعُوذُ بِكَ رَبِّ أَن يَحْضُرُونِ

**Transliteration:** *Rabbi a'udhu bika min hamazatish-shayatin wa a'udhu bika Rabbi an yahdurun*

**Source:** Quran 23:97-98 (Quran) | **Repeat:** 3x

📖 A Quranic protection from Shaytan's whispers and his very presence. Allah commanded the Prophet (SAW) to say this.

---


### 💫 sad
**Sad** (حزين) — *Seek comfort and hope*

#### 1. I seek forgiveness from Allah the Almighty, there is no deity except Him, the Ever-Living, the Sustainer, and I repent to Him

> أَسْتَغْفِرُ اللَّهَ الْعَظِيمَ الَّذِي لَا إِلَٰهَ إِلَّا هُوَ الْحَيُّ الْقَيُّومُ وَأَتُوبُ إِلَيْهِ

**Transliteration:** *Astaghfirullaha al-'Azeem alladhi la ilaha illa huwa al-Hayy al-Qayyum wa atubu ilayh*

**Source:** Sunan Abu Dawud 1517 (Hadith) | **Repeat:** 3x

📖 The Prophet (SAW) said whoever says this, Allah will forgive him even if he fled from battle.

---

#### 2. O Allah, I seek refuge in You from worry and grief, from weakness and laziness, from cowardice and miserliness, and from being overcome by debt and overpowered by men

> اللَّهُمَّ إِنِّي أَعُوذُ بِكَ مِنَ الْهَمِّ وَالْحَزَنِ، وَأَعُوذُ بِكَ مِنَ الْعَجْزِ وَالْكَسَلِ، وَأَعُوذُ بِكَ مِنَ الْجُبْنِ وَالْبُخْلِ، وَأَعُوذُ بِكَ مِنْ غَلَبَةِ الدَّيْنِ وَقَهْرِ الرِّجَالِ

**Transliteration:** *Allahumma inni a'udhu bika minal-hammi wal-hazan, wa a'udhu bika minal-'ajzi wal-kasal, wa a'udhu bika minal-jubni wal-bukhl, wa a'udhu bika min ghalabatid-dayni wa qahrir-rijal*

**Source:** Sahih Bukhari 6369 (Hadith) | **Repeat:** 3x

📖 The Prophet (SAW) used to frequently make this comprehensive dua seeking refuge from worry, sadness, and all forms of distress.

---

#### 3. Indeed we belong to Allah and to Him we shall return. O Allah, reward me in my calamity and replace it with something better

> إِنَّا لِلَّهِ وَإِنَّا إِلَيْهِ رَاجِعُونَ، اللَّهُمَّ أْجُرْنِي فِي مُصِيبَتِي وَأَخْلِفْ لِي خَيْرًا مِنْهَا

**Transliteration:** *Inna lillahi wa inna ilayhi raji'un. Allahumma'jurni fi musibati wa akhlif li khayran minha*

**Source:** Sahih Muslim 918 (Hadith) | **Repeat:** 3x

📖 Umm Salamah (RA) said this when her husband died, and Allah replaced him with the Prophet (SAW) as her husband.

---

#### 4. All praise is due to Allah in every circumstance

> الْحَمْدُ لِلَّهِ عَلَى كُلِّ حَالٍ

**Transliteration:** *Alhamdulillahi 'ala kulli hal*

**Source:** Sunan Ibn Majah 3803 (Hadith) | **Repeat:** 3x

📖 Said when faced with something disliked. Teaches us to be grateful in all circumstances.

---

#### 5. I seek forgiveness from Allah

> أَسْتَغْفِرُ اللَّهَ

**Transliteration:** *Astaghfirullah*

**Source:** Sahih Bukhari 6307 (Hadith) | **Repeat:** 100x

📖 The Prophet (SAW) used to seek forgiveness from Allah more than 70 times a day.

---

#### 6. O Allah, I have wronged myself greatly and none forgives sins except You. Grant me forgiveness from Yourself and have mercy on me. You are the Forgiving, the Merciful

> اللَّهُمَّ إِنِّي ظَلَمْتُ نَفْسِي ظُلْمًا كَثِيرًا وَلَا يَغْفِرُ الذُّنُوبَ إِلَّا أَنْتَ، فَاغْفِرْ لِي مَغْفِرَةً مِنْ عِنْدِكَ وَارْحَمْنِي إِنَّكَ أَنْتَ الْغَفُورُ الرَّحِيمُ

**Transliteration:** *Allahumma inni zalamtu nafsi zulman kathiran wa la yaghfirudh-dhunuba illa anta, faghfir li maghfiratan min 'indika warhamni innaka antal-Ghafurur-Rahim*

**Source:** Sahih Bukhari 834 (Hadith) | **Repeat:** 3x

📖 The Prophet (SAW) taught Abu Bakr (RA) to say this in his prayer. A comprehensive dua for forgiveness.

---

#### 7. My Lord, do not leave me alone, and You are the best of inheritors

> رَبِّ لَا تَذَرْنِي فَرْدًا وَأَنتَ خَيْرُ الْوَارِثِينَ

**Transliteration:** *Rabbi la tadhirni fardan wa anta khayrul-warithin*

**Source:** Quran 21:89 (Quran) | **Repeat:** 3x

📖 The dua of Prophet Zakariya (AS). Excellent for those feeling alone or longing for companionship.

---

#### 8. O Allah, forgive all my sins, the small and the great, the first and the last, the public and the private

> اللَّهُمَّ اغْفِرْ لِي ذَنْبِي كُلَّهُ، دِقَّهُ وَجِلَّهُ، وَأَوَّلَهُ وَآخِرَهُ، وَعَلَانِيَتَهُ وَسِرَّهُ

**Transliteration:** *Allahummaghfir li dhanbi kullahu, diqqahu wa jillahu, wa awwalahu wa akhirahu, wa 'alaniyatahu wa sirrahu*

**Source:** Sahih Muslim 483 (Hadith) | **Repeat:** 3x

📖 The Prophet (SAW) would make this comprehensive dua for forgiveness in his sujud, covering all types of sins.

---

#### 9. Our Lord, we have wronged ourselves, and if You do not forgive us and have mercy upon us, we will surely be among the losers

> رَبَّنَا ظَلَمْنَا أَنفُسَنَا وَإِن لَّمْ تَغْفِرْ لَنَا وَتَرْحَمْنَا لَنَكُونَنَّ مِنَ الْخَاسِرِينَ

**Transliteration:** *Rabbana zalamna anfusana wa in lam taghfir lana wa tarhamna lanakoonanna minal-khasirin*

**Source:** Quran 7:23 (Quran) | **Repeat:** 7x

📖 The first dua of repentance by Adam and Hawwa (AS). Allah taught them these words to seek forgiveness.

---

### 💫 grateful
**Grateful** (شاكر) — *Express your thankfulness*

#### 1. All praise is due to Allah, by Whose grace good deeds are completed

> الْحَمْدُ لِلَّهِ الَّذِي بِنِعْمَتِهِ تَتِمُّ الصَّالِحَاتُ

**Transliteration:** *Alhamdulillahil-ladhi bi ni'matihi tatimmus-salihat*

**Source:** Sunan Ibn Majah 3803 (Hadith) | **Repeat:** 3x

📖 Said when something good happens. The Prophet (SAW) taught this expression of gratitude for blessings.

---

#### 2. All praise is due to Allah in every circumstance

> الْحَمْدُ لِلَّهِ عَلَى كُلِّ حَالٍ

**Transliteration:** *Alhamdulillahi 'ala kulli hal*

**Source:** Sunan Ibn Majah 3803 (Hadith) | **Repeat:** 3x

📖 Said when faced with something disliked. Teaches us to be grateful in all circumstances.

---

#### 3. Our Lord, give us good in this world and good in the Hereafter, and protect us from the punishment of the Fire

> رَبَّنَا آتِنَا فِي الدُّنْيَا حَسَنَةً وَفِي الْآخِرَةِ حَسَنَةً وَقِنَا عَذَابَ النَّارِ

**Transliteration:** *Rabbana atina fid-dunya hasanatan wa fil-akhirati hasanatan wa qina 'adhaban-nar*

**Source:** Quran 2:201 (Quran) | **Repeat:** 3x

📖 One of the most comprehensive duas in the Quran. The Prophet (SAW) used to make this dua frequently.

---

#### 4. Glory be to Allah and praise Him

> سُبْحَانَ اللَّهِ وَبِحَمْدِهِ

**Transliteration:** *Subhanallahi wa bihamdihi*

**Source:** Sahih Bukhari 6405 (Hadith) | **Repeat:** 100x

📖 Whoever says this 100 times in the morning and evening will have his sins forgiven even if they were like the foam of the sea.

---

#### 5. Glory be to Allah, all praise is due to Allah, none has the right to be worshipped except Allah, and Allah is the Greatest

> سُبْحَانَ اللَّهِ، وَالْحَمْدُ لِلَّهِ، وَلَا إِلَٰهَ إِلَّا اللَّهُ، وَاللَّهُ أَكْبَرُ

**Transliteration:** *Subhanallah, wal-hamdulillah, wa la ilaha illallah, wallahu akbar*

**Source:** Sahih Muslim 2137 (Hadith) | **Repeat:** 33x

📖 The four beloved phrases to Allah. More beloved to the Prophet (SAW) than everything the sun rises upon.

### 🌙 upon-waking
**Upon Waking** (عند الاستيقاظ) — *Thank Allah for a new day*

#### 1. We have entered the morning and the dominion belongs to Allah. All praise is due to Allah. None has the right to be worshipped except Allah, alone, without partner. To Him belongs the dominion and to Him belongs all praise, and He is over all things capable

> أَصْبَحْنَا وَأَصْبَحَ الْمُلْكُ لِلَّهِ، وَالْحَمْدُ لِلَّهِ، لَا إِلَٰهَ إِلَّا اللَّهُ وَحْدَهُ لَا شَرِيكَ لَهُ، لَهُ الْمُلْكُ وَلَهُ الْحَمْدُ وَهُوَ عَلَى كُلِّ شَيْءٍ قَدِيرٌ

**Transliteration:** *Asbahna wa asbahal-mulku lillah, wal-hamdulillah, la ilaha illallahu wahdahu la sharika lah, lahul-mulku wa lahul-hamdu wa huwa 'ala kulli shay'in qadir*

**Source:** Sunan Abu Dawud 5068 (Hadith) | **Repeat:** 1x

📖 One of the morning adhkar. Establishes tawheed and gratitude at the start of each day.

---

#### 2. All praise is due to Allah Who has given us life after causing us to die, and to Him is the resurrection

> الْحَمْدُ لِلَّهِ الَّذِي أَحْيَانَا بَعْدَ مَا أَمَاتَنَا وَإِلَيْهِ النُّشُورُ

**Transliteration:** *Alhamdulillahil-ladhi ahyana ba'da ma amatana wa ilayhin-nushur*

**Source:** Sahih Bukhari 6312 (Hadith) | **Repeat:** 1x

📖 Said immediately upon waking. Acknowledging Allah's power over life and death.

---


### 🌙 during-illness
**During Illness** (عند المرض) — *Seek healing and patience*

#### 1. O Allah, grant me health in my body. O Allah, grant me health in my hearing. O Allah, grant me health in my sight. None has the right to be worshipped except You

> اللَّهُمَّ عَافِنِي فِي بَدَنِي، اللَّهُمَّ عَافِنِي فِي سَمْعِي، اللَّهُمَّ عَافِنِي فِي بَصَرِي، لَا إِلَٰهَ إِلَّا أَنْتَ

**Transliteration:** *Allahumma 'afini fi badani, Allahumma 'afini fi sam'i, Allahumma 'afini fi basari, la ilaha illa ant*

**Source:** Sunan Abu Dawud 5090 (Hadith) | **Repeat:** 3x

📖 A morning dua asking Allah for health in body, hearing, and sight - our most important faculties.

---

#### 2. O Allah, I ask You for pardon and well-being in this world and the Hereafter

> اللَّهُمَّ إِنِّي أَسْأَلُكَ الْعَفْوَ وَالْعَافِيَةَ فِي الدُّنْيَا وَالْآخِرَةِ

**Transliteration:** *Allahumma inni as'alukal-'afwa wal-'afiyata fid-dunya wal-akhirah*

**Source:** Sunan Ibn Majah 3871 (Hadith) | **Repeat:** 3x

📖 The Prophet (SAW) said: 'Ask Allah for pardon and well-being, for after certainty of faith, no one is given anything better than well-being.'

---

#### 3. O Allah, Lord of mankind, remove the hardship. Heal, for You are the Healer. There is no healing except Your healing, a healing that leaves no illness behind

> اللَّهُمَّ رَبَّ النَّاسِ أَذْهِبِ الْبَاسَ، اشْفِ أَنْتَ الشَّافِي، لَا شِفَاءَ إِلَّا شِفَاؤُكَ، شِفَاءً لَا يُغَادِرُ سَقَمًا

**Transliteration:** *Allahumma Rabban-nas, adhhibil-ba's, ishfi antash-Shafi, la shifa'a illa shifa'uk, shifa'an la yughadiru saqama*

**Source:** Sahih Bukhari 5675 (Hadith) | **Repeat:** 3x

📖 The Prophet (SAW) would visit the sick and make this dua for them. A powerful supplication for healing.

---

#### 4. In the name of Allah I perform ruqyah for you, from everything that harms you, from the evil of every soul or envious eye. May Allah heal you. In the name of Allah I perform ruqyah for you

> بِسْمِ اللَّهِ أَرْقِيكَ، مِنْ كُلِّ شَيْءٍ يُؤْذِيكَ، مِنْ شَرِّ كُلِّ نَفْسٍ أَوْ عَيْنِ حَاسِدٍ، اللَّهُ يَشْفِيكَ، بِسْمِ اللَّهِ أَرْقِيكَ

**Transliteration:** *Bismillahi arqik, min kulli shay'in yu'dhik, min sharri kulli nafsin aw 'aynin hasid, Allahu yashfik, bismillahi arqik*

**Source:** Sahih Muslim 2186 (Hadith) | **Repeat:** 3x

📖 The ruqyah (spiritual healing) that Jibreel performed on the Prophet (SAW).

---


### 🌙 traveling
**Traveling** (السفر) — *Protection for your journey*

#### 1. In the name of Allah, with whose name nothing can harm on earth or in heaven, and He is the All-Hearing, All-Knowing

> بِسْمِ اللَّهِ الَّذِي لَا يَضُرُّ مَعَ اسْمِهِ شَيْءٌ فِي الْأَرْضِ وَلَا فِي السَّمَاءِ وَهُوَ السَّمِيعُ الْعَلِيمُ

**Transliteration:** *Bismillahil-ladhi la yadurru ma'asmihi shay'un fil-ardi wa la fis-sama'i wa huwas-Sami'ul-'Alim*

**Source:** Sunan Abu Dawud 5088 (Hadith) | **Repeat:** 3x

📖 Whoever says this three times in morning and evening, nothing will harm them.

---

#### 2. In the name of Allah, I place my trust in Allah. There is no power or strength except with Allah

> بِسْمِ اللَّهِ، تَوَكَّلْتُ عَلَى اللَّهِ، لَا حَوْلَ وَلَا قُوَّةَ إِلَّا بِاللَّهِ

**Transliteration:** *Bismillah, tawakkaltu 'alallah, la hawla wa la quwwata illa billah*

**Source:** Sunan Abu Dawud 5095 (Hadith) | **Repeat:** 1x

📖 Said when leaving the house. One who says this is guided, protected, and defended, and Satan will stay away.

---

#### 3. Glory be to Him Who has subjected this to us, and we could never have it by our efforts. And to our Lord we shall return

> سُبْحَانَ الَّذِي سَخَّرَ لَنَا هَذَا وَمَا كُنَّا لَهُ مُقْرِنِينَ وَإِنَّا إِلَى رَبِّنَا لَمُنْقَلِبُونَ

**Transliteration:** *Subhanal-ladhi sakhkhara lana hadha wa ma kunna lahu muqrinin, wa inna ila Rabbina lamunqalibun*

**Source:** Quran 43:13-14 (Quran) | **Repeat:** 1x

📖 The Quranic dua for traveling. Acknowledging that all means of transportation are from Allah.

---

#### 4. O Allah, we ask You in this journey of ours for righteousness, piety, and deeds that please You

> اللَّهُمَّ إِنَّا نَسْأَلُكَ فِي سَفَرِنَا هَذَا الْبِرَّ وَالتَّقْوَى، وَمِنَ الْعَمَلِ مَا تَرْضَى

**Transliteration:** *Allahumma inna nas'aluka fi safarina hadhal-birra wat-taqwa, wa minal-'amali ma tarda*

**Source:** Sahih Muslim 1342 (Hadith) | **Repeat:** 1x

📖 Part of the comprehensive travel supplication taught by the Prophet (SAW).

---

#### 5. I seek refuge in the perfect words of Allah from the evil of what He has created

> أَعُوذُ بِكَلِمَاتِ اللَّهِ التَّامَّاتِ مِنْ شَرِّ مَا خَلَقَ

**Transliteration:** *A'udhu bi kalimatillahit-tammati min sharri ma khalaq*

**Source:** Sahih Muslim 2708 (Hadith) | **Repeat:** 3x

📖 The Prophet (SAW) said whoever says this upon stopping somewhere, nothing will harm them until they leave.

---


### 🌙 facing-difficulty
**Facing Difficulty** (عند الشدة) — *Strength through hardship*

#### 1. Sufficient for us is Allah, and He is the best Disposer of affairs

> حَسْبُنَا اللَّهُ وَنِعْمَ الْوَكِيلُ

**Transliteration:** *Hasbunallahu wa ni'mal wakeel*

**Source:** Quran 3:173 (Quran) | **Repeat:** 7x

📖 Said by Prophet Ibrahim (AS) when thrown into fire, and by the Companions before the Battle of Badr. Brings immense peace during anxiety and fear.

---

#### 2. There is no deity except You; exalted are You. Indeed, I have been of the wrongdoers

> لَا إِلَٰهَ إِلَّا أَنتَ سُبْحَانَكَ إِنِّي كُنتُ مِنَ الظَّالِمِينَ

**Transliteration:** *La ilaha illa anta subhanaka inni kuntu minaz-zalimin*

**Source:** Quran 21:87 (Quran) | **Repeat:** 3x

📖 The supplication of Prophet Yunus (AS) in the belly of the whale. Allah says no Muslim will call upon Him with this except that He will respond.

---

#### 3. Indeed we belong to Allah and to Him we shall return. O Allah, reward me in my calamity and replace it with something better

> إِنَّا لِلَّهِ وَإِنَّا إِلَيْهِ رَاجِعُونَ، اللَّهُمَّ أْجُرْنِي فِي مُصِيبَتِي وَأَخْلِفْ لِي خَيْرًا مِنْهَا

**Transliteration:** *Inna lillahi wa inna ilayhi raji'un. Allahumma'jurni fi musibati wa akhlif li khayran minha*

**Source:** Sahih Muslim 918 (Hadith) | **Repeat:** 3x

📖 Umm Salamah (RA) said this when her husband died, and Allah replaced him with the Prophet (SAW) as her husband.

---

#### 4. All praise is due to Allah in every circumstance

> الْحَمْدُ لِلَّهِ عَلَى كُلِّ حَالٍ

**Transliteration:** *Alhamdulillahi 'ala kulli hal*

**Source:** Sunan Ibn Majah 3803 (Hadith) | **Repeat:** 3x

📖 Said when faced with something disliked. Teaches us to be grateful in all circumstances.

---

#### 5. O Allah, there is no ease except what You make easy, and You make the difficulty easy if You wish

> اللَّهُمَّ لَا سَهْلَ إِلَّا مَا جَعَلْتَهُ سَهْلًا، وَأَنْتَ تَجْعَلُ الْحَزْنَ إِذَا شِئْتَ سَهْلًا

**Transliteration:** *Allahumma la sahla illa ma ja'altahu sahla, wa anta taj'alul-hazna idha shi'ta sahla*

**Source:** Sahih Ibn Hibban 2427 (Hadith) | **Repeat:** 7x

📖 A powerful dua for when facing any difficulty. Reminds us that only Allah can make things easy.

---

#### 6. O Ever-Living, O Sustainer, in Your mercy I seek relief

> يَا حَيُّ يَا قَيُّومُ بِرَحْمَتِكَ أَسْتَغِيثُ

**Transliteration:** *Ya Hayyu ya Qayyumu bi rahmatika astaghith*

**Source:** Jami at-Tirmidhi 3524 (Hadith) | **Repeat:** 3x

📖 The Prophet (SAW) taught this dua for times of distress. Calling upon Allah by His beautiful names.

---

#### 7. O Allah, suffice me against them however You will

> اللَّهُمَّ اكْفِنِيهِمْ بِمَا شِئْتَ

**Transliteration:** *Allahummak-finihim bima shi't*

**Source:** Sahih Muslim 3005 (Hadith) | **Repeat:** 3x

📖 Said when fearing harm from others. Entrusting one's affairs entirely to Allah.

---

#### 8. There is no power or strength except with Allah

> لَا حَوْلَ وَلَا قُوَّةَ إِلَّا بِاللَّهِ

**Transliteration:** *La hawla wa la quwwata illa billah*

**Source:** Sahih Bukhari 6384 (Hadith) | **Repeat:** 10x

📖 One of the treasures from beneath the Throne of Allah. Said when feeling powerless or overwhelmed.

---

#### 9. Our Lord, pour upon us patience and plant firmly our feet and give us victory over the disbelieving people

> رَبَّنَا أَفْرِغْ عَلَيْنَا صَبْرًا وَثَبِّتْ أَقْدَامَنَا وَانصُرْنَا عَلَى الْقَوْمِ الْكَافِرِينَ

**Transliteration:** *Rabbana afrigh 'alayna sabran wa thabbit aqdamana wansurna 'alal-qawmil-kafirin*

**Source:** Quran 2:250 (Quran) | **Repeat:** 3x

📖 The dua of the believers when facing Goliath. Asking for patience and steadfastness.

---

#### 10. My Lord, indeed I am, for whatever good You would send down to me, in need

> رَبِّ إِنِّي لِمَا أَنزَلْتَ إِلَيَّ مِنْ خَيْرٍ فَقِيرٌ

**Transliteration:** *Rabbi inni lima anzalta ilayya min khayrin faqir*

**Source:** Quran 28:24 (Quran) | **Repeat:** 7x

📖 The dua of Prophet Musa (AS) when he was alone and in need. Shortly after, Allah blessed him with shelter, family, and provision.

---

#### 11. Our Lord, forgive us our sins and our excess in our affairs and plant firmly our feet and give us victory

> رَبَّنَا اغْفِرْ لَنَا ذُنُوبَنَا وَإِسْرَافَنَا فِي أَمْرِنَا وَثَبِّتْ أَقْدَامَنَا وَانصُرْنَا عَلَى الْقَوْمِ الْكَافِرِينَ

**Transliteration:** *Rabbanaghfir lana dhunubana wa israfana fi amrina wa thabbit aqdamana wansurna 'alal-qawmil-kafirin*

**Source:** Quran 3:147 (Quran) | **Repeat:** 3x

📖 The dua of the righteous warriors who fought alongside the prophets. Combines repentance with asking for steadfastness.

---

**Transliteration:** *Wa qul Rabbi zidni 'ilma*

**Source:** Quran 20:114 (Quran) | **Repeat:** 7x

📖 Allah commanded the Prophet (SAW) to ask for increase only in knowledge. Knowledge is the greatest treasure.

---

**Source:** Sunan Ibn Majah 925 (Hadith) | **Repeat:** 3x

📖 The Prophet (SAW) would say this after Fajr prayer. Asking for the three pillars of a successful life.
