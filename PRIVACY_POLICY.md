# Privacy Policy for Dhikir App

**Last Updated:** January 15, 2026

## Introduction

Dhikir ("we," "our," or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our iOS application.

## Summary

**Dhikir collects NO personal data. All information stays on your device.**

## Information We Do NOT Collect

- Personal identification information (name, email, phone)
- Location data
- Usage analytics or tracking data
- Device identifiers
- Cookies or similar technologies
- Health or biometric data
- Financial information
- Contact lists or address books

## Information Stored Locally on Your Device

The following data is stored **only on your device** and is never transmitted to us or any third party:

- **Reading History**: Which dhikirs you have viewed
- **Favorites**: Dhikirs you have marked as favorites
- **Streak Data**: Your daily usage streak for personal motivation
- **Preferences**: Language selection, appearance mode, notification settings
- **Notification Times**: Your preferred reminder times

This data is stored using Apple's SwiftData framework and remains entirely on your device. If you delete the app, this data is permanently removed.

## Local Notifications

If you enable notifications, the app schedules **local notifications** on your device. These notifications:
- Are generated and delivered entirely by your device
- Do not require an internet connection
- Do not transmit any data to external servers
- Can be disabled at any time in the app settings or iOS Settings

## Third-Party Services

Dhikir does **not** use any third-party services, including:
- Analytics platforms (Google Analytics, Firebase, etc.)
- Advertising networks
- Social media integrations
- Cloud storage services
- Crash reporting services

## Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, rent, or trade any user information with third parties because we do not collect any user information.

## Children's Privacy

Dhikir does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13. The app is suitable for users of all ages.

## Data Security

Since all data remains on your device and is never transmitted, your information is protected by your device's built-in security features, including:
- iOS data encryption
- Device passcode/Face ID/Touch ID protection
- App sandboxing

## Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:
- **Access**: View your data within the app (history, favorites, settings)
- **Delete**: Use the "Reset All Data" option in Settings, or delete the app
- **Portability**: Your data is stored locally and can be included in device backups
