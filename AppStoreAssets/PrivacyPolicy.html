Privacy Policy for Dhikir

Last updated: January 15, 2026

Introduction

Dhikir ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our iOS application.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information.

Dhikir is designed with privacy as a core principle. The app:

  • Does not require account creation or login
  • Does not collect your name, email, or any personal identifiers
  • Does not track your location
  • Does not use analytics or tracking services
  • Does not contain advertisements
  • Does not share any data with third parties

Data Stored on Your Device

The app stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Favorites: Dhikirs you mark as favorites
  • Reading History: A log of dhikirs you have read
  • Streak Data: Your daily usage streak count
  • Settings: Your app preferences (language, notifications, haptic feedback)

This data is stored using Apple's SwiftData framework and remains entirely on your device. We cannot access this data, and it is not transmitted anywhere.

Push Notifications

If you enable push notifications, the app will send you local reminders at times you configure. These notifications are generated entirely on your device and do not involve any external servers or data transmission.

Third-Party Services

Dhikir does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, or advertising networks.

Data Security

Your data is protected by the security measures built into iOS. Since all data stays on your device and is never transmitted, there is no risk of data breaches from our end.

Children's Privacy

Dhikir does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13 years of age. The app is suitable for users of all ages.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

  • GitHub: github.com/AhmedHamadto/Dhikir/issues

Your Rights

Since we don't collect any personal data, there is no data for us to provide, modify, or delete. All your data is stored locally on your device and can be removed by uninstalling the app.

This privacy policy is effective as of January 15, 2026.
