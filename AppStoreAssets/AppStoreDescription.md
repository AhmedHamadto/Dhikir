## Description

**Find the right words to connect with Allah, no matter how you're feeling.**

Dhikir brings you authentic Islamic remembrances (adhkar) from the Quran and Hadith, thoughtfully organized by your emotional state and life situations. Whether you're feeling anxious, grateful, sad, or hopeful, find the perfect dua to bring peace to your heart.

**Features:**

• **Emotion-Based Selection** - Choose from 10 emotional states including anxious, sad, grateful, hopeful, and more. Each emotion has carefully selected duas to help you through that moment.

• **Authentic Sources** - Every dhikir is sourced from the Quran or authentic Hadith collections (Bukhari, Muslim, Tirmidhi). No fabricated or weak narrations.

• **Arabic + Translation** - View the original Arabic text, transliteration for pronunciation, and translations in multiple languages (English, Urdu, Indonesian, Turkish, French, Malay).

• **Repetition Counter** - Track your dhikir count with a beautiful circular progress indicator. Haptic feedback helps you stay focused.

• **Favorites** - Save your most meaningful duas for quick access anytime.

• **Reading History** - Track your spiritual journey with a complete history of your readings.

• **Daily Streak** - Build a consistent habit of remembrance with streak tracking.

• **Share** - Share beautiful duas with family and friends.

• **Offline First** - Works completely offline. Your spiritual companion, anywhere.

• **Dark Mode** - Easy on your eyes during night prayers.

**Why Dhikir?**

In moments of difficulty, finding the right words can be hard. Dhikir removes that barrier by presenting you with exactly the right remembrance for your situation. The Prophet ﷺ taught us specific duas for specific moments - this app makes that knowledge accessible at your fingertips.

Start your journey of remembrance today. As Allah says: "Verily, in the remembrance of Allah do hearts find rest." (Quran 13:28)

## App Review Information

### Notes for Reviewer

Dhikir is a simple, offline Islamic remembrance app. No account or login is required. Here is a quick walkthrough:

1. Launch the app. You will see the Home screen with emotion and life situation categories.
2. Tap any category (e.g. "Anxious" or "Grateful") to view curated duas (supplications) from the Quran and Hadith.
3. Swipe left/right to browse through the duas. Tap the counter button at the bottom to track repetitions.
4. Tap the heart icon to save a dua to Favorites. Tap the share icon to share it.
5. Use the bottom tab bar to navigate to Favorites, History, and Settings.
6. In Settings you can toggle haptic feedback, enable daily reminder notifications, and switch between light/dark mode.

The app is entirely offline. It does not connect to any server, collect any data, or require any sign-in. All content is bundled within the app and sourced from the Quran and authenticated Hadith collections.
